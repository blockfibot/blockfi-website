Non-US Client Estate Distribution Status and Next Steps FAQs

What is the current status of estate distributions for non-US clients?

As of November 27, 2024, non-US Clients who have not yet received their estate distributions must comply with Bermuda’s regulatory requirements, which mandate completion of a KYC remediation process.
Many non-US clients have completed the process and have already received their funds. However, there are clients who have not yet completed the remediation process. We strongly encourage all customers who have not received their distributions or finished this process to complete this process prior to the deadline.

What is the deadline for completing the remediation process?

You must complete the remediation process by May 27, 2025 11:59 PM UTC.

Why do I need to remediate my account?

To comply with Bermuda’s regulatory requirements, non-US clients are required to successfully complete a remediation process in order to receive their outstanding BlockFi estate distribution.

How do I remediate my account?

There are two ways to verify your identity:

  1. You can access a link to our identity verification provider “Persona” directly through our website by following these steps:
    1. Navigate to blockfi.com
    2. Click the “KYC Remediation” button on the top menu.
    3. Enter your Unique ID in the KYC Remediation email you received from blockfi@e.blockfi.com 
    4. Follow the instructions on the Persona portal to complete the process. At the end, you should see a green check mark that indicates your remediation is submitted correctly. 
  1. Alternatively, you may access your identity verification link in the email you received from no-reply@frompersona.com with the subject line “Verify your identity”.
    * Note: Due to previous phishing attempts targeting BlockFi clients, we advise confirming the “from” address and subject line match what is listed above before clicking the link in the email and following the instructions provided on the portal to complete the process.

Ensure to have all required documents ready before you start the process and complete the session once you begin. Once you start the process, the link will expire within 24 hours of when you click “begin.”

If your link expires due to the process not being completed within the allotted timeframe, a new link will be issued to you within a few days.

What documents do I need to have ready to successfully complete the remediation process?

The KYC Remediation process will have two parts: 

  1. Identity Verification: You’ll need your passport and another government-issued ID. You may also be required to provide documents to verify address, if not included in the government-issued ID.
  2. W-8 Form Submission: You’ll be required to submit the completed W-8 Form during the verification process, which is mandatory for you to receive your outstanding BlockFi estate distribution.
    We ask you to download and complete the form prior to starting the verification process. Download the W-8 Form using this link: https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw8ben.pdf
    IRS Instructions for W-8: https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/iw8ben.pdf

    The W-8 portion of the portal can accept any document. If you are required by tax authorities to provide a W-9 instead of a W-8, please do so accordingly to complete the final portion of the remediation process.

How long will the remediation process take to complete?

The remediation process is expected to take approximately 10 minutes.

When can I expect to receive my outstanding estate distribution?

It may take approximately 45 days after your submission has been reviewed and approved to process your payment.
BlockFi will reach out to you if we need additional documentation to approve your account. 

What if I experience issues during the remediation process?

If we determine that your submission is incomplete, we may either reach out for additional information or you may be given a chance to complete the process again.

Additional information requests will be sent directly through our Identity Verification provider “Persona” from the email address blockfi@cases.withpersona.com.

If you receive an email requesting additional information, ensure the email address matches exactly as stated above before following the provided instructions.

What if I fail to complete the remediation process?

If you fail to successfully complete the process by the deadline you will not be eligible to receive your outstanding estate distribution.

How can I verify that the email I received is a legitimate communication from BlockFi?

Due to past phishing attempts and scams targeting BlockFi clients, we’ve posted a list of official channels used by BlockFi and its partners to communicate with clients directly on our website: blockfi.com/blockfi-official-client-outreach-channels/
If you’re unsure if a communication is legitimate, please review the ‘from’ address of the email against the official email list provided.
